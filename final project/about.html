Who is Ink?

Inkcess, commonly referred to as Ink is a digital cartoon artist who occassionally works with traditional media. She is currently working on her B.Tech and is hence not taking commissions year round any longer. Ink dabbles in character design and world building and hopes to one day write and/or illustrate stories geared towards those learning to read in English, particularly young children.
